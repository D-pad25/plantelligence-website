Plantelligence

Your Smart Planter Tackling Reforestation at its Root

Our Mission

Plantelligence is developing SPROUT, an autonomous steep-terrain planting robot, to solve Australia’s critical reforestation labour bottleneck. Built for Commercial Plantations, NGOs, Agroforestry, and Carbon Offset initiatives, our platform enables high-throughput planting in even the toughest environments.

Why It Matters

  • Australia must plant 1 billion trees by 2030—manual labour can't meet demand due to shortages and injury risks.
  • SPROUT plants up to 6,000 saplings per 8-hour shift with ≥75% germination accuracy and steep-slope capability.
  • Autonomous, modular, safe, and IP67 rated—our robot is engineered for real-world forestry conditions.

Market Opportunity

Our beachhead market—Commercial Plantations—represents a $1B TAM with a 170% CAGR driven by national tree-planting targets. Plantelligence aims to scale across global forestry and reforestation sectors, addressing climate and biodiversity goals.

Our Values

  • Reforest with Purpose: Driving Australia's One-Billion-Tree goal with smart automation.
  • Precision Innovation: Autonomous, GPS-guided planting with real-time monitoring and analytics.
  • Safety & Stewardship: IP-rated, terrain-safe design protects users, trees, and ecosystems.
  • Partnership-Powered Growth: Built alongside plantation managers, NGOs, and sustainability leaders.
